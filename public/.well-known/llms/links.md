## Recommended Collector Resources

### 130point
- **URL**: https://www.130point.com/cards
- **Category**: Price Research
- **Description**: The go-to resource for sports card pricing and market data. Essential for comping cards before buying or selling.
- **Use Case**: Check current market values based on recent sales

### Card Ladder
- **URL**: https://www.cardladder.com
- **Category**: Portfolio Tracking
- **Description**: Track card prices over time and manage your collection portfolio. Great for monitoring market trends.
- **Use Case**: Long-term price tracking and collection management

### Checklist Insider
- **URL**: https://www.checklistinsider.com
- **Category**: Product Checklists
- **Description**: Comprehensive sports card checklists and set information. Know exactly what cards exist in every product.
- **Use Case**: Research products before breaking or buying

### MMA Rookies
- **URL**: https://www.mmarookies.com
- **Category**: UFC News
- **Description**: The premier source for UFC card collecting news, fighter profiles, rookie checklists, and MMA card market analysis.
- **Use Case**: Stay updated on UFC fighters and card releases

### TCDB (Trading Card Database)
- **URL**: https://www.tcdb.com/ViewAll.cfm/sp/MMA?MODE=Years
- **Category**: Card Database
- **Description**: Browse every MMA card set ever released by year. The complete historical archive of all MMA trading cards.
- **Use Case**: Research historical sets, find specific cards, explore the complete MMA card history

## Why These Resources?

These are the tools we actually use at Chicken1of1:

1. **Price Research** - 130point for current market values
2. **Portfolio Tracking** - Card Ladder for monitoring investments
3. **Product Knowledge** - Checklist Insider for understanding sets
4. **UFC News** - MMA Rookies for fighter updates affecting card values
5. **Historical Research** - TCDB for exploring the complete MMA card archive
